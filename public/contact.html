Before You Contact Us

Check these common questions for instant answers

How do I track my order?

You can track your order status by logging into your account or using the order tracking link sent to your email.

When will I receive my tickets?

Digital tickets are delivered instantly after payment confirmation. Physical tickets are sent 7-10 days before the event.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed automatically if there are delivery issues. For other refund requests, contact support with your order number.

What if my tickets don't work at the venue?

Contact our emergency support line immediately. We provide 24/7 assistance for venue entry issues and guarantee resolution.
