Pricing FAQ

Common questions about our fees and pricing

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we believe in complete transparency. All fees are clearly displayed before you complete your purchase.

When are seller fees deducted?

Seller commission fees are automatically deducted when payment is released after successful ticket delivery.

Can I get a refund on service fees?

If a transaction is cancelled due to seller issues, all fees including service fees are fully refunded to the buyer.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, high-volume sellers may qualify for reduced commission rates. Contact our sales team for enterprise pricing.
