Protection Policy FAQ

Common questions about buyer protection

Is there a time limit to file a protection claim?

You must report issues within 48 hours of discovering the problem, or within 24 hours after the event for entry-related issues.

What if the seller disputes my claim?

Our neutral dispute resolution team reviews all evidence from both parties and makes a fair decision based on our protection policy terms.

Are there any fees for filing a protection claim?

No, filing a protection claim is completely free. We never charge buyers for dispute resolution services.

What happens if I can't attend the event anymore?

Our protection policy covers seller issues, not buyer changes of plans. However, you can try to resell your tickets through our platform.
