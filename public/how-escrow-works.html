How Our Escrow Service Works

Your money stays protected until tickets are safely delivered. Here's exactly how our escrow process keeps every transaction secure.

1

Buyer Makes Payment

When you purchase tickets, your payment is immediately transferred to our secure escrow account, not to the seller. Your money is held safely while we verify the transaction.

Your payment is protected from the moment you click "Buy" • the seller cannot access your money until delivery is confirmed.

Payment Secured

Funds held in escrow account

Seller Notified

Verification process begins

2

Seller Verification

The seller receives notification of your purchase and must provide proof of ticket ownership. Our team verifies the tickets are legitimate and match your order details.

All sellers must pass identity verification and provide valid ticket proof before any transaction proceeds.

3

Secure Ticket Transfer

Once verified, the seller transfers tickets directly to you through secure digital delivery. You receive confirmation and can verify the tickets in your account.

Tickets are transferred instantly through secure digital delivery, no waiting for physical mail or risky meetups.

Tickets Delivered

Secure digital transfer complete

Payment Released

Seller receives funds

Transaction Complete

Only after you confirm receipt of valid tickets do we release payment to the seller. The transaction is complete and both parties are protected throughout the entire process.

Your satisfaction is guaranteed · if anything goes wrong, our dispute resolution team ensures a fair outcome.
