Terms of Service

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing and using SecureTickets ("we," "us," or "our"), you accept and agree to be bound by the terms and provision of this agreement. If you do not agree to abide by the above, please do not use this service.

2. Service Description

SecureTickets is an online marketplace that facilitates secure ticket transactions between buyers and sellers using escrow protection. We provide a platform for ticket listings, secure payment processing, and dispute resolution services.

3. User Responsibilities

Buyers must:

  • Provide accurate payment information
  • Review ticket details before purchase
  • Report issues within specified timeframes
  • Use tickets for personal use only

Sellers must:

  • Complete identity verification process
  • Provide legitimate, transferable tickets
  • Deliver tickets as promised
  • Respond to buyer communications promptly

4. Escrow Protection

All transactions are protected by our escrow service. Buyer payments are held securely until ticket delivery is confirmed. Funds are released to sellers only after successful completion of the transaction or dispute resolution.

5. Fees and Payments

Service fees are clearly disclosed before transaction completion. Buyers pay a 3.5% service fee plus payment processing costs. Sellers pay a 5.5% commission fee. All fees are non-refundable except in cases of service failure.

6. Prohibited Activities

Users may not:

  • List or sell counterfeit, invalid, or stolen tickets
  • Engage in fraudulent or deceptive practices
  • Violate any applicable laws or regulations
  • Interfere with platform security or functionality
  • Create multiple accounts to circumvent restrictions

7. Dispute Resolution

Disputes are resolved through our internal mediation process. Users agree to participate in good faith and accept our final decisions. Legal action may only be pursued after exhausting our dispute resolution process.

8. Limitation of Liability

SecureTickets' liability is limited to the transaction amount. We are not responsible for event cancellations, venue changes, or other circumstances beyond our control. Our maximum liability per incident is $5,000.

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better user experience by remembering your preferences and enabling essential site functionality.
